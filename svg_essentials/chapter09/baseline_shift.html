SVG baseline-shift

This is a demonstration of SVG's baseline-shift which allows for the presentation of superscript and subscript in text elements. The example here is based on a similar example in chapter 9 of 'SVG Essentials' by J. David Eisenberg.

In the present example the text looks somewhat clumsy. In practice the text for the superscript and subscript elements might sensibly be reduced in size.

Please see accompanying code.
