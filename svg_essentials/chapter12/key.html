SVG keyPoints, keyTimes, and calcMode

An example of the use of keyPoints, keyTimes, and calcMode when animating an SVG. The calcMode determines how the overall timing of the animation is conducted. The keyPoints attribute allows certain points during the animation to be designated and tied to a change in pace using the keyTimes attribute.

This example is based on a similar example in chapter 12 of 'SVG Essentials' by J. David Eisenberg.
