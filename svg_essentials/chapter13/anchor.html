SVG with anchor element

A demonstration of the use of the anchor element within an SVG canvas. In this case the word SOLOLEARN links to the home page of sololearn's site, the instagram and facebook icons link to the home pages of instagram and facebook and the two geometric shapes link to www.w3/SVG/.

The two media icons can be viewed at https://code.sololearn.com/W76DfcE1dyQk/#html

This example is based on a similar example in chapter 13 of 'SVG Essentials' by J. David Eisenberg.

Please see the accompanying code.
